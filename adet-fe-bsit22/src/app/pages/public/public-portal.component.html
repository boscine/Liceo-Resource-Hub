Digital Privacy & Scholarly Safeguarding

Institutional protocols for the safeguarding of scholarly data and student identities within the Liceo de Cagayan University ecosystem.

1. Scholarly Data Collection

Following the Data Privacy Act of 2012, we collect essential institutional metadata to facilitate academic resource sharing. This includes your university-issued email address (`@liceo.edu.ph`), name, and profile descriptors provided during registration.

2. Redaction & Transparency

Your academic identity is handled with utmost discretion. Contact information is redacted for unauthenticated visitors and is only revealed to verified fellow scholars when a "Cooperation Offer" is explicitly accepted. No data is shared with third-party commercial entities.

3. Archival Security

We utilize industry-standard encryption and secure JWT-based authentication to protect your archival records. Access to the portal is strictly governed by institutional credentials to prevent unauthorized data extraction.

Terms of Scholarly Exchange & Ethics

The ethical framework and digital code of conduct governing peer-to-peer material sharing within our institution.

1. Scholar Eligibility

Access is strictly limited to verified students and faculty of Liceo de Cagayan University. Use of non-institutional email domains for archival purposes is prohibited. Accounts found to be "squatting" or impersonating scholars will be purged.

2. Material Integrity & Prohibited Content

All listings must serve a legitimate scholarly purpose. Prohibited items include illegal substances, non-academic tools, or materials that violate intellectual property rights. Academic dishonesty (e.g., selling test answers) is strictly forbidden and will be reported to the Dean.

3. Digital Conduct

Members must maintain the "Curator's Standard"—professional communication and reliable cooperation. Repeated failure to fulfill scholarly agreements or harassment of fellow scholars will result in immediate suspension from the Hub.
