Borrowing Ethics

Liceo Resource Hub is built on peer-to-peer accountability. Respect the stipulated duration of use and return items in their original condition.

verified

Item Condition Standards

  • Like New: Unused or perfectly maintained books/tools.
  • Functional: Clear wear but 100% usable for academic work.
  • End-of-Life: Heavily damaged; strictly for parts or reference.
shield_person

Institutional Security

Prohibited: Sharing external contact info beyond the provided "Messenger/Phone" system. Personal identity theft or external commercial solicitations will result in immediate bans.
