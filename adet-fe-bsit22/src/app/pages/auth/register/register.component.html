Terms of Scholarly Exchange

1. Institutional Scope

The Liceo Resource Hub is exclusively for currently enrolled students of Liceo de Cagayan University. Access is granted solely through verified institutional emails.

2. Scholarly Conduct

Members agree to use the platform for the exchange of educational materials, tools, and scholarly resources only. Any commercial or non-academic use is prohibited.

3. Accountability

Users are responsible for the accuracy of their requests and the condition of resources exchanged. The platform facilitates connections but is not liable for individual peer-to-peer agreements.

4. Privacy & Data

Contact information is restricted to verified scholars and only revealed upon a request's detailed inquiry. We never share institutional data with external entities.
