Liceo Regulation V1.2

Digital Privacy & Data Protection

Institutional protocols for the safeguarding of scholarly data and student identities.

1. Scholarly Data Collection

We collect essential institutional metadata to facilitate academic resource sharing. This includes your university-issued email address, collegiate affiliation, and profile descriptors provided during registration.

2. Information Usage

Your data is processed exclusively to maintain the integrity of the Liceo Resource Hub. Contact information is redacted for unauthenticated visitors and is only revealed to verified peers during "Scholarly Cooperation" offers.

3. Archival Security

We utilize hardware-accelerated encryption and secure JWT-based authentication to protect your archival records. Access to the portal is strictly governed by institutional credentials.

Archive Protocol V2.0

Terms of Scholarly Exchange

The ethical framework governing peer-to-peer material sharing within the institution.

1. Scholar Eligibility

Access is strictly limited to verified students of Liceo de Cagayan University with an active `@liceo.edu.ph` domain. Suspended or banned accounts lose immediate access to all archival features.

2. Material Integrity

Users are prohibited from listing illegal, inappropriate, or non-educational materials. The institutional moderator maintains the right to purge any request that violates scholarly standards.

3. Liability & Responsibility

The Liceo Resource Hub is a curation platform. All physical exchanges and cooperation occur outside the digital interface. The institution is not liable for the condition of physical materials exchanged.
